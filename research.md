<ul>

</li>
<li>Achieved a CAGR of 24.5% and sharpe ratio of 2.17 with RL model, compared to DJIâ€™s CAGR of 13.57% and sharpe ratio of 1.15. </li>

<h3 id="content"> <li> Option Pricing </li></h3>
<ul>

difference, monte carlo simulation and numerical integration methods.
